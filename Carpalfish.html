Carpalfish

The Carpalfish is a Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Brine Pool. It will swim like most fish and charge at the player at a fast speed after building-up the attack. It will charge at the player even if separated by a wall as long as the player is submerged in water. If the Carpalfish collides with a solid surface while attempting to hit the player, it has a chance to drop a Venom Fang. Like all enemies in the Brine Pool, it will attack anything that isn't a member of its species.
