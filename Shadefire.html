Shadefire

Shadefire is a debuff that is applied by some Corruption-themed items as a pre-Hardmode version of the Shadowflame debuff. When applied to an NPC, they will lose 7.5 life per second. If also affected by the Oiled debuff the affected NPC will instead receive 32.5 damage every second. When applied to a player, the player will lose 2 life per second.
