Messy Leech

The Messy Leech is a pre-Hardmode accessory that can be fished for in the Jungle. It counts as a common item and has a 10% chance of being obtained if the requisites are met. While equipped, all attacks from the player inflict Bleeding.

Crafting

Used In

Tips

- Obtaining the Messy Leech early is a good investment for Defiled worlds as the Bleeding debuff prevents Defiled enemies from regenerating after stealing mana from the player. This effect also works on the Defiled Amalgamation and could make its fight easier.
