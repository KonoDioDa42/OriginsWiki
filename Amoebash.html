Amoebash

The Amoebash is a Hardmode melee weapon that has a 1/5 (20%) chance of being dropped by Riven Mimics. Upon colliding with an enemy or surface after being swung, it will release damaging amebic droplets that inflict Torn for 1.34 seconds at a severity of 15%. The hammer itself also inflicts Torn for 2 seconds at a severity of 40%.

Its best modifier is Legendary.
