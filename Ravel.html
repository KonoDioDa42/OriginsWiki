Ravel

Ravel is a Hardmode accessory that has a 1/5 (20%) chance of dropping from a Defiled Mimic. Ravel turns the player into a small sphere by double-tapping the Down button, which provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. Ravel has a different role as an accessory in that it can be upgraded and combined into different variations providing unique abilities while ravelled.
