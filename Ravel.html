Ravel

Ravel is a Hardmode mount that has a 1/5 (20%) chance of dropping from a Defiled Mimic. Ravel turns the player into a small sphere by double-tapping the Down button, which provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. Ravel has a different role as a mount in that it can be upgraded and combined into different variations providing unique abilities while ravelled. Ravel is also used to navigate through smaller veins and Ravel mazes in the underground Defiled Wastelands.
