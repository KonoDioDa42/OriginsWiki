Journal

The Journal is a mechanic added by Origins as a means to keep track of quests and information from whispered items. Whispered items contain hidden information that is unlocked usually after defeating the enemies or obtaining the item associated with them. The Journal has four tabs: the 'Index', 'Quests', 'Notes' and 'Search' tabs. Index sorts all recorded item entries in alphabetical order, Quests displays all active and completed quests in alphabetical order, Notes allows you to write in the Journal and the Search tab allows the player to search for an item entry or quest.
