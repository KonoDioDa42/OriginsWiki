Unstrung Fiberglass Bow

The Unstrung Fiberglass Bow is a pre-Hardmode bow that has a 1/10 (10%) chance to be dropped by Enchanted Fiberglass Bows. It behaves like any other bow except that it must be strung up by using Vines. Each vine strung to it adds 25 shots before it needs to be strung again for a maximum capacity of 50 shots.

Its best modifier is Deadly or Heavy Caliber.
