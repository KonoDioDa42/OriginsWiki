Acetabularia

Acetabularia is a plant grown on Riven Grass or Riven Jungle Grass blocks found around the Riven Hive. It is the Riven-equivalent to the Corruption's Vile Mushroom and the Crimson's Vicious Mushroom.

Trivia

- Acetabularia is based off of a real type of green algae with the same name Acetabularia. It is also sometimes called the "mermaid's wineglass" since it resembles a cup.
