Gray Ice Block

Gray Ice Block is a block type found in a Snow biome converted by the Defiled. It is the Defiled-equivalent to ice blocks, and can be converted to other variants with any thrown powder.

Notes

- The Gray Ice Block can be purified, but will quickly reclaim lost ground. This is a part of the Defiled Resurgence mechanic where the Defiled cannot be cleansed until all Defiled Hearts are removed from the world.
