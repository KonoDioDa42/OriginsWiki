Petrified Prickly Pear

The Petrified Prickly Pear is a food item that has a 3/100 (3%) chance to drop from Petrified Trees when shaken. When consumed, it provides the Well Fed buff and 25% increased mana shielding, which lasts for 5 minutes.

Notes

- Petrified Prickly Pear's effect is stackable with other mana shielding boosts (see item synergies). - Mana shielding takes into account the damage the player would have received with 0 defense and 0% damage reduction. This results in the proper amount of damage reduced from the attack, but an inefficiency in the mana consumed from it. - Whenever the player benefits from mana shielding, Mana Buffer will be applied preventing any mana regeneration for 3.2 seconds.
