Drill Ravel

Drill Ravel is a Hardmode mount that is crafted using a Ancient Chisel and a Ravel. Drill Ravel turns the player into a small sphere, upon double-tapping the Down button, that provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. Drill Ravel also allows the player to utilize any pickaxe or drill in their inventory with a 25% increase to their mining speed.
