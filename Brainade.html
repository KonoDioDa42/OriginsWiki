Brainade

The Brainade is an explosive crafted using Crimtane Ore and Grenades. They are also sold by the Demolitionist NPC for 250 after selling him 81 Peat Moss once the Brain of Cthulhu has been defeated. Brainades explode into 12-18 blood droplets that inflict Bleeding for 3 seconds and Confused for 2 seconds.
