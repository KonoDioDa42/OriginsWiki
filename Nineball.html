Nineball

The Nineball is a melee weapon that is crafted with Sanguinite Bars.

Its best modifier is Godly or Demonic.

Trivia

- The Nineball's stats and recipe only requiring 9 Sanguinite Bars is a deliberate reference to Nine-Ball from the Armored Core franchise. Its sprite looks very close to Nine-Ball's insignia as well.
