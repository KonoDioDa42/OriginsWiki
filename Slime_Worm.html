Slime Worm

The Slime Worm is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Underground and Cavern layers or anywhere in the world during a Slime Rain event. It follows worm AI except that it splits into multiple of the color of slime it's derived from on death.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Slime Worm: "What's better than one slime? Multiple slimes stacked on top of each other! What's better than multiple slimes stacked on top of each other? A SLIME WORM!"
