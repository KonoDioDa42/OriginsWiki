Protean Potion

The Protean Potion is a buff potion that can be crafted using Bottled Water, a Tearracuda and any evil herb. When consumed, it provides the Protean buff, which increases the player's stat share by 50%. This effect will last for 4 minutes, but can be canceled anytime by right-clicking the icon.

Tips

- The Protean Potion has a unique item synergy involving the player filling all of their accessory slots with all of the class-specific emblems and then using a Protean Potion to amplify all of the damage for all weapons regardless of class. This playstyle will require the player to farm the Wall of Flesh at minimum of 8 times for all of the possible emblems (including the combo accessories) and fish in the Riven Hive for an extended period in order to have enough potions.
