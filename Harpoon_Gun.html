Harpoon Gun

If you were looking for the article with a list of all harpoon guns added to Origins, click here.

The Harpoon Gun is a pre-Hardmode weapon that has a 1/200 (0.5%) chance of being dropped by most enemies during a Goblin Army. It is functionally the same as the vanilla Harpoon it replaces except that it now requires harpoons for ammunition. The harpoon gun and weapons like it have a small chance of a line and harpoon being lost when colliding with a tile or enemy.

Its best modifier is Godly or Demonic.
