Powerflower

Powerflower is a pre-Hardmode accessory combining a Mana Flower with a Dim Starlight. The Powerflower offers an ample supply of mana for mana-hungry builds by releasing stars from critical hits, reducing mana consumption by 8% and automatically using Mana Potions when needed. Like the Dim Starlight and its other upgrades, the Powerflower emits light.
