Black Hole Bomb

The Black Hole Bomb is a type of post-Moon Lord explosive that can be obtained from the Elderforge after completing the Test of the Ancients. The Black Hole Bomb remains in place after landing on a tile or enemy and generates a growing black hole that has an incredible pull force that pulls in any entities and items nearby it. While expanding, the black hole will deal 10% of its base damage to any enemies inside of it. After expanding for a short time, the black hole will collapse dealing impressive damage and destroying any tiles within its blast radius.

Tips

- The Black Hole Bomb is one of the best Demolitionist weapons in Origins due to its ability to pull enemies towards it. However, it is especially effective against worm-type enemies where it can damage all of the segments with the same damage during its growth and eventual explosion.
- On the contrary, Black Hole Bombs suffer against bosses that share a similar design to Plantera, and will instead be pushed back with the same amount of force used to pull them in.
