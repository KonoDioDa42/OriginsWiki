Cicada 3301

Cicada 3301 is a critter found in the Defiled Wastelands when the player breaks any background objects found there. It can be caught with a Bug Net to be carried around in the inventory and released later, or crafted into animated decorative furniture (Cicada 3301 Cage). Once crafted, the Cicada 3301 cannot be released again, unless the furniture item is de-crafted via Shimmer. Cicada 3301 can furthermore be used as bait for fishing, with a bait power of 40%.

Trivia

- Cicada 3301 was inspired by the internet puzzle with the same name, with its purpose still a mystery to this day.
- The Bestiary entry for the Cicada 3301: "This isn't the 3301st cicada in the Defiled's possession, this is the 3301st iteration the Defiled has created cicadas much like it. It is the perfect shape."
