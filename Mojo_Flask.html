Mojo Flask

The Mojo Flask is a consumable, potion-like item sold by the Dryad NPC at all times. When consumed, it provides the Purifying buff, which cleanses 20% of all Assimilation types for each of its 5 uses. The Mojo Flask will go on cooldown for 5 seconds after each use through the Mojo Sickness debuff.

Unlike most consumables, the Mojo Flask will not be removed from the player's inventory once it reaches all of its uses. Instead it will remain empty until the player refills it by right-clicking a Cleansing Station while this is being held.
