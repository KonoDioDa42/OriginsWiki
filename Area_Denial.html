Area Denial

The Area Denial is a pre-Hardmode magic weapon that is obtained through the Broken Record. It launches a probe to the targetted location the player selects on screen and explodes in 1-second intervals once it has stopped, dealing considerable damage and knockback in a large blast radius. Only one probe can be active at a time and it can be blocked from its destination by tiles in the way, bouncing off of them. Once placed, the probe will remain for five minutes, similarly to the Crimson Rod. Like the Shimmer Rod, probes can be canceled by using the RMB button while Area Denial is being held.

Its best modifier is Demonic or Deadly for damage output, and Loaded for its knockback and blast radius.
