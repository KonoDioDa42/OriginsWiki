Stone Mask

The Stone Mask is a pre-Hardmode accessory that can be found anywhere on the surface of the world as a tile. Only one Stone Mask spawns in a world unless in some world seeds. It increases the player's defense by 8, but decreases the player's movement speed by 10% and their jump speed by 33%.
