I-WTPA Standard

The I-WTPA Standard is a pre-Hardmode accessory that has a 1/4 (25%) chance of being dropped by Undead Miners found Underground. It is also sold by the Demolitionist NPC for 50000 after selling him 35 Peat Moss. While equipped, all explosive fuse times will be reduced by 33%.
