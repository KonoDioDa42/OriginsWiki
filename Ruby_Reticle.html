Ruby Reticle

The Ruby Reticle is a Hardmode accessory crafted with Cursed Flames, Rubies and Carburite. It increases the player's critical strike chance by 15% of the current held weapon's base damage.

Tips

- Ruby Reticle is strong with weapons that have a high base damage and tends to be weaker with rapid-firing, low damage weapons.
