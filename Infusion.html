Infusion

Infusion is a pre-Hardmode magic weapon that has a 1/5 (20%) chance of being dropped by destroyed Defiled Fissures in a Defiled Wastelands. It also has a 1/5 (20%) chance of being obtained from Pointy and Bilious Crates fished in the Defiled. It rapidly fires needles that stick to enemies and explode after enough stick the same enemy.

Its best modifier is Mythical.
