Eitrite Bar

Eitrite Bars are Hardmode bars crafted using Eitrite Ore at a Adamantite/Titanium Forge. They are used to craft early hardmode equipment and weapons. Eitrite bars are an alternative to Adamantite and Titanium Bars.
