Dew Justice

The Dew Justice is a Hardmode magic weapon that has a 1/5 (20%) chance to be dropped by destroyed Riven Mimics. Using it summons amebic dew from surfaces nearby the cursor, which propel themselves towards the cursors location at the time of when it was first summoned. The amebic dew infclits the Slow debuff for 4 seconds.

The best modifier is Mythical.
