Spider Ravel

Spider Ravel is a Hardmode mount that is crafted using Cobwebs, Spider Fangs and Ravel. Spider Ravel turns the player into a small sphere that provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. Spider Ravel allows the player to latch onto walls and ceilings by pressing the corresponding key whereas Move Left is used to remain attached to a wall directly left of the player or Up is used to remain attached to a ceiling the player can touch.
