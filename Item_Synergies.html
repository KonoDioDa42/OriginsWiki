Item synergies

Item synergies are any combination of accessories, armor, consumables and weapons that all benefit from each other to promote a unique way of playing the game. Some item synergies are more powerful than others and some offer completely different purposes during gameplay. Below is a list of all notable synergies, their strategies and their methods of achieving.
