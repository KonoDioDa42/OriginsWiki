Tripod Nip

Tripod Nip is a Hardmode accessory that has a 1/48 (2.083%) chance of being dropped by a Defiled Tripod. While equipped, enemy aggression is decreased by 400 and spawn rates are decreased. Additionally, Defiled Tripods will be friendly to the player with this item equipped.

Trivia

- The existence of Tripod Nip inquires that Defiled Tripod behavior is inherently cat-like.
