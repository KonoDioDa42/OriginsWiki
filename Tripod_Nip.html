Tripod Nip

Tripod Nip is a Hardmode accessory that has a 1/48 (2.083%) chance of being dropped by a Defiled Tripod. While equipped, enemy aggression is decreased by 400 and spawn rates are decreased. Additionally, Defiled Tripods will be friendly to the player holding this item.

Tripod Nip is a lore item indicated by "You hear chittering nearby..." unlike similar items which are indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."

Trivia

- The existence of Tripod Nip inquires that Defiled Tripod behavior is inherently cat-like. This is reinforced by its lore entry.
