Blotopus

The Blotopus is a pre-Hardmode gun that can be fished for in the Crimson. It is the Crimson-equivalent to Corruption's Purple Clubberfish. It rapidly fires needles out of its mouth that are affected by gravity and inflict Bleeding.

Its best modifier is Demonic or Deadly.
