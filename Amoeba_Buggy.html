Amoeba Buggy

The Amoeba Buggy is a critter found in the Riven Hive when the player breaks any background objects found there or when destroying a Barnacle Mound. It can be caught with a Bug Net to be carried around in the inventory and released later, or crafted into animated decorative furniture (Amoeba Buggy Cage). Once crafted, the Amoeba Buggy cannot be released again, unless the furniture item is de-crafted via Shimmer. The Amoeba Buggy can furthermore be used as bait for fishing, with a bait power of 40%.
