Glass Cannon

The Glass Cannon is a pre-Hardmode launcher that has a 1/5 (20%) chance to be dropped by the Fiberglass Weaver. It launches 4 canisters in a spread slowly that each deal their full amount of damage.

Its best modifier is Rapid.

Tips

- It is most effective the closer the player is to the target, and while some form of self-damage protection is in use. This is so the player can prevent each of the launched canisters from being too spread out.
