Conehead Zombie

The Conehead Zombie is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns on the surface at night. It is better protected against damage due to the Traffic Cone on its head. It has no other behavior from other Zombies.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Conehead Zombie: "Conehead Zombie shuffled mindlessly forward like every other zombie. But something made him stop, made him pick up a traffic cone and stick it on his head. Oh yeah. He likes to party."
- As hinted at by its bestiary entry, the Conehead Zombie is inspired by an enemy of the same likeness in Plants vs Zombies.
