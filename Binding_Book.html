Binding Book

The Binding Book is a pre-Hardmode accessory that is crafted with Celestial Cuffs and Refactoring Pieces. While this is equipped, the player will have 10% increased mana shielding, an increased pickup range for Stars and an increased mana capacity by 20. Additionally, if Lost armor is not equipped, the player will also receive a 5% increase in damage reduction.

Notes

- Binding Book's effect is stackable with other mana shielding boosts (see item synergies).
- Mana shielding takes into account the damage the player would have received with 0 defense and 0% damage reduction.
This results in the proper amount of damage reduced from the attack, but an inefficiency in the mana consumed from it. - Whenever the player benefits from mana shielding, Mana Buffer will be applied preventing any mana regeneration for 3.2 seconds.
- The Binding Book does not stack with Magic Cuffs or any of its variants for decreased mana consumption.
