Amoeba Hook

The Amoeba Hook is a Hardmode hook that has a 1/5 (20%) chance of being dropped by Riven Mimics. The Amoeba Hook has no resistance when the player has reached its latch point resulting in a wobble.

Tips

- A perfectly-timed hook cancel right when the player reaches the latch point of the Amoeba Hook could result in a speed boost.
