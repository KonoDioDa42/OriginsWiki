The Kruncher

The Kruncher is a pre-Hardmode gun that is always dropped on the first destroyed Defiled Fissure (along with 100 Musket Balls) in a Defiled Wastelands and has a 1/5 (20%) chance of dropping from destroyed Fissures afterward. It also has a 1/5 (20%) chance of being obtained from Crunchy and Bilious Crates fished in the Defiled. The Kruncher acts like a rapid-firing shotgun, firing clusters of 3-5 shots at a time. It also has a unique sound effect from other weapons that it shares with its ancient version: the Ancient Kruncher.

Its best modifier is Unreal.
