Low Signal

The Low Signal is a pre-Hardmode magic weapon that is dropped by the Defiled Amalgamation. It slowly fires a projectile spray similarly to the Amalgamation that explodes into spikes after some time or when coming into contact with an enemy or surface. It is particularly effective against flying enemies.

Its best modifier is Mythical.

Lore

- Upon obtaining a Low Signal, the following will appear in the Journal:

"The towers constructed to ensure synchrony. Many minds fighting for one will, survival everlasting as we understand common enemies and learn from borrowed experiences."
This can be found in order of relevance in The Defiled lore page.
