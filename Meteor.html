Meteor

The Meteor is a pre-Hardmode launcher that can be crafted using Meteorite Bars. The Meteor converts its ammunition into miniature meteorites that are always shot upwards regardless of where the player aims. This allows the Meteor to target areas that cannot be reached through direct fire. Upon impact with an enemy or tile, the meteorite will explode initially but remain in place wherever it landed for 3 seconds before exploding again and destroying tiles similarly to Bombs. The Meteor additionally has guaranteed critical hits against flying enemies while it is falling.

Its best modifier is Guileless for any statistic other than velocity. For velocity, Heavy-duty is recommended so the projectile doesn't have to travel as high up to reach its target destination, which is particularly ideal for enclosed spaces.
