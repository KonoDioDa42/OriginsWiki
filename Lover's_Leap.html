Lover's Leap

Lover's Leap is a pre-Hardmode accessory combining Spectre Boots with a Locket Necklace. It allows the player to run fast while having a 20% increase in damage and 50% increase in movement speed after taking damage. It also allows the player to dash by double-tapping the horizontal movement keys and to temporarily propel themselves vertically after a jump by holding the jump key.

The dash provided by Lover's Leap can damage an enemy the player comes in contact with and knock them back.
