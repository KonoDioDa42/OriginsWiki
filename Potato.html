Potato

The Potato is a food item that has a 1/34 (2.94%) chance to drop from enemies during the Goblin Army event, 1/13 chance to be dropped by any zombie or Harpy, and is guaranteed to drop from Nymphs. When consumed, it provides the Well Fed buff, which lasts for 10 minutes. It can be placed by right-clicking above any soils.
