Fiberglass Undergrowth

"They call it the Jungle's Window not because it is made out of glass, but because it is clear just how hostile its machinations are."

The Fiberglass Undergrowth is a mini-biome found in the Underground Jungle somewhere below the Brine Pool. It is comprised entirely of Fiberglass Blocks and Walls, and contains enemies themed around it. It generates as a wide oval that replaces all of the tiles with its own where it generated, not adding any custom structures.

The Undergrowth is very dangerous as its enemies have some level of stealth as nearly all of them remain still until they detect the player. The enemies in this biome take the form of weapons enchanted by the biome to attack the player. Enemies such as the Enchanted Fiberglass Cannon are even capable of inflicting lethal damage to some players.
