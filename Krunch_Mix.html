Krunch Mix

The Krunch Mix is a food item that has a 1/17 (5.88%) chance of being dropped by a Defiled Phantom. When consumed, it provides the Well Fed and Regeneration buffs, which last for 6 minutes.

Tips

- When farming for Krunch Mix, be up high in the Defiled Wastelands and make sure visible ground is limited to prompt more Defiled Phantoms to spawn.

Trivia

- An inside joke of Krunch Mix, "A candy fair to the average white, male child", was under consideration as a tooltip for some time but was not chosen. The context is that Krunch Mix resembles the Defiled in appearance and was referred as "Skittles for colorblind people" while the most colorblind group of people statisically are white, male children, hence the phrase.
