Suspicious Looking Pebble

The Suspicious Looking Pebble is a light pet-summoning item that spawns a floating Defiled Blight that follows the player around and emits a dim, white light. The Defiled Blight can move through solid objects and liquids unhindered. If it falls far enough behind, it will teleport to the player's location. It is the Defiled counterpart to the Corruption's Shadow Orb and the Crimson's Crimson Heart.
