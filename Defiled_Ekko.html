Defiled Ekko

The Defiled Ekko is an enemy found in the Defiled Wastelands all throughout the game after the player has reached 400 max life. Defiled Ekkoes follow the behaviors of Town NPCs, except with hostility towards the player and various weapons they could use. Defiled Ekkoes can use their own version of the Infusion, Dismay and Bone Latcher that each scale with difficulty. In Master Mode, Defiled Ekkoes gain the benefits of Mysterious Spray once Hardmode is reached.

Tips

- The best way to handle Defiled Ekkoes is to build barriers to avoid them if possible, however, if a confrontation is necessary, use explosives to make haste of them. Like most Defiled enemies, they are prone to high bursts of damage.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Defiled Ekko: "An attempt for the Defiled to copy the abilities of the Terrarian, this being serves as a huge threat to anything that is cleasning the Defiled."
