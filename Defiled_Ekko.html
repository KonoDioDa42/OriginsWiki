Defiled Ekko

The Defiled Ekko is an enemy found in the Defiled Wastelands all throughout the game after the player has reached 400 max life. Defiled Ekkoes follow the Fighter AI, except with being able to use various weapons and their own version of the Chunky Hook at their disposal. Defiled Ekkoes can use their own version of the Infusion, Dismay and Bone Latcher that each scale with difficulty. In Master Mode, Defiled Ekkoes gain the benefits of Mysterious Spray once Hardmode is reached.
