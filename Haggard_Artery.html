Haggard Artery

The Haggard Artery is a pre-Hardmode accessory combining an Explosive Artery with a Messy Leech. While equipped, enemies inflicted with Bleeding within 24 tiles of the player have an 1/30 (3.33%) chance to explode every second. Additionally, all enemies attacked by the player are inflicted Bleeding (including explosions from this item).
