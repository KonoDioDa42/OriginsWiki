Scavenger Bag

The Scavenger Bag is a Hardmode post-Plantera accessory combining the effects of the Papyrus Scarab and Keepsake Remains. While equipped, the player's armor penetration is increased by 5, max number of minions by 1, summon damage by 15% and the knockback of minions by 2. Additionally, all of the player's attacks inflict Torn.
