Sunflower Seed Shooter

The Sunflower Seed Shooter is a pre-Hardmode gun that can be crafted using Sunflowers and Wood. It is meant to be the starter gun for rangers with its easy crafting recipe and inexpensive ammunition source of Sunflowers. It has a modest fire rate and a 33% chance not to consume ammo.

Its best modifier is Demonic or Deadly.
