Blast-Resistant Plate

The Blast-Resistant Plate is a Hardmode accessory crafted using Adamantite Bars or Titanium Bars with Obsidian. It protects the player by reducing self-damage by 20% and providing 3 defense. It is also a material used to craft the Blast armor set.
