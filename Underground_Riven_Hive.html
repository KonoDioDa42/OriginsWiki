Underground Riven Hive

"An ecosystem the Primordial Amoeba forced into existence so it could eat away at the world."

The Underground Riven Hive is a biome that is an underground version of the surface-level Riven Hive, appearing at and below the Cavern layer. Just like the surface-based Riven Hive, it is mainly composed of Spug Flesh and spreads to adjacent tiles. Although the inital Hive reaches deep enough to access it in pre-Hardmode, most of its exclusive contents will only appear when the world enters Hardmode. After the Wall of Flesh is defeated for the first time, a diagonal stripe of Riven is generated, creating additional Underground Hives.

The Underground Riven Hive does not suppress the spawning of other enemies in the underground or Cavern layer. During Hardmode, all enemies killed in the Underground Hive have a 20/36% chance to drop a Soul of Night.

Note: Most characteristics of the Riven Hive also apply to the Underground Riven Hive. See the Riven Hive for more extensive information.
