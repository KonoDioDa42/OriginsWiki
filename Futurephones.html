Futurephones

Futurephones are an Hardmode, post-Mechanicalaccessory combining Headphones and Sniper's Mark. While equipped, all attacks (other than from a minion) will be able to tag enemies. Additionally, generic damage is increased by 5% and generic critical strike chances by 10% against tagged enemies.
