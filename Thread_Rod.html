Thread Rod

The Thread Rod is a pre-Hardmode summon weapon that has a 1/10 (10%) chance to be dropped by the Fiberglass Weaver. Using it summons up to 5 threads that deal damage and inflict the Slow debuff on enemies that come into contact with them. The threads summoned by this count as sentries and have a maximum length of 64 tiles. The player can also remove summoned threads by pressing the Interact button while the Thread Rod is selected in the hotbar.

Its best modifier is Mythical.
