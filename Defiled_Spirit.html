Defiled Spirit

Defiled Spirits are consumable thrown explosives dropped by Defiled Wisps that spawn after killing Defiled enemies in Expert Mode. They are also sold by the Demolitionist NPC for 200 after selling him 50 Peat Moss once the Defiled Amalgamation has been defeated. Defiled spirits can additionally be used as ammunition for the Snowball Cannon, giving them greater velocity. Defiled spirits explode on impact and have a decent blast radius.
