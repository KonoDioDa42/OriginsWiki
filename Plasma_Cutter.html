Plasma Cutter

The Plasma Cutter is a Hardmode magic weapon found in Riven Dungeon Chests. It fires an even cluster of shots that each apply Torn as a cumulative effect, with each applying it at a severity of 30%. The Plasma Cutter applies more Torn and therefore deals more damage with better aim from the player. It deals low damage for a Hardmode weapon at first, but consistent hits could easily make the Plasma Cutter quite effective as it benefits from the effects twice as much.

The best modifier is Mythical.
