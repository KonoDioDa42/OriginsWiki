Sea Dragon

The Sea Dragon is a Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Brine Pool. The Defiled Phantom will quickly swim towards the player and attack them. When the player leaves water while being chased after a Sea Dragon, it will jump out of water to reach them. Getting hit by a Sea Dragon inflicts Acid Venom for 2-3 seconds and Toxic Shock for 3-4. Like all enemies in the Brine Pool, it will attack anything that isn't a member of its species.
