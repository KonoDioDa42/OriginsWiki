Bone Latcher

The Bone Latcher is a pre-Hardmode melee weapon that has a 1/38 (2.63%) chance of being dropped by Defiled Cyclopes, or can be crafted using Defiled Bars. The Bone Latcher behaves like a whip despite being a melee weapon except that it pulls enemies that are 12 or greater tiles away from the player towards the player instead of knocking them back.

Its best modifier is Legendary. Modifiers that affect weapon knockback instead increase the beckoning force of the Bone Latcher.

Tips

- The Bone Latcher has an excellent synergy with Soulhide Armor due to its ability to pull enemies toward you. Combined with Soulhide Armor, you can wither enemies down by inflicting them with the weaker Shadowflame debuff provided by Soulhide's set bonus.

Trivia

- The Bone Latcher as it was planned was originally going to be planned for the release of Origins before Terraria 1.4 came out. Origins was never released before 1.4 or even during 1.4, so it behaves like a melee-ified whip as of now.
