Ichor Canister

The Ichor Canister is a type of Hardmode canister used as ammunition for launchers and the Proximity Mine Launcher. Upon landing on a tile or enemy, the Ichor Canister will explode and have a chance to inflict the Ichor debuff to any enemies within its blast radius. Directly hit enemies will instead be guaranteed to be receive a longer lasting Ichor debuff.
