Unmisfire

The Unmisfire is a pre-Hardmode bow that can be crafted using Defiled Bars. Arrows fired from the bow will glitch out and teleport with their velocity unaltered in order to hit enemies that would've been near misses. Arrows that land on the ground raise a defiled spike in their wake.

Its best modifier is Mythical.
