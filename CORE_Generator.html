Core Generator

The CORE Generator is a Hardmode accessory that combines the Last Descendant and Missile Armcannon. Swinging while this is equipped will fire any rockets in the player's inventory from their chest. Additionally, equipping the Armcannon provides these benefits:

- +30% explosive velocity
- +10% explosive attack speed
- +15% explosive blast radius
- +50% explosive knockback
- Auto-use on all explosive weapons
- +20% generic damage, +50% movement speed and doubled length of invincibility after taking damage
- Rapidly release canisters from your inventory and stars will fall from the sky after taking damage
