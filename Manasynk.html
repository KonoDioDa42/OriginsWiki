Manasynk

The Manasynk is a pre-Hardmode magic weapon that can be obtained by fishing in the Defiled Wastelands. Throwing the fish causes it to latch onto enemies and steal mana, turning it into gray 'Defiled Mana' Star pick-ups. Defiled Mana stars regenerate the player's mana according to how much damage the Manasynk deals. The Manasynk is an alternative option to mana replishment in pre-Hardmode and is infinitely reusable so long as the player has enough mana.

Its best modifier is Mythical.

Trivia

- The Manasynk is based off of the sea lamprey fish.
