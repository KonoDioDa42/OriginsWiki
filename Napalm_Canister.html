Napalm Canister

The Napalm Canister is a type of pre-Hardmode canister used as ammunition for launchers and the Proximity Mine Launcher. Upon landing on a tile or enemy, the Napalm Canister will explode into napalm that deals damage over time to any enemies that walk over it. The Napalm Canister's napalm spreads the thermite released by the Thermite Launcher when both are used together.
