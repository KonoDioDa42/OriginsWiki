Ancient Defiled Cyclops

The Ancient Defiled Cyclops is a pre-Hardmode enemy that rarely spawns in the Defiled Wastelands. The Ancient Defiled Cyclops takes on the appearance of one of the older iterations of the Defiled Cyclops, standing roughly 8-tiles tall. With the exception of life regen and any of the noises Defiled enemies typically make, it behaves the exact same as regular Defiled Cyclopes. Ancient Defiled Cyclopes are a good source for Ancient Defiled armor as the armor set is 38 times more likely to be dropped from it than other Defiled enemies.
