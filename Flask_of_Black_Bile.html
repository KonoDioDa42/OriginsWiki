Flask of Black Bile

The Flask of Black Bile is a Hardmode flask that can be crafted using Bottled Water and Black Bile. When consumed, it allows all melee weapons and whips to inflict the Rasterized debuff for 0.4 seconds. This effect will last for 20 minutes, but can be canceled anytime by right-clicking the icon. Melee weapons that fire projectiles will also inflict the debuff on enemies hit by the projectiles (except for the Flairon's bubbles).

The Flask of Black Bile cannot be used in conjunction with other flasks, and using it will remove any existing flask effects. Like all other flasks, the effect will not be canceled when the player dies.
