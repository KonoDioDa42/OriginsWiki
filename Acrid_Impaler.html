Acrid Impaler

The Acrid Impaler is a Hardmode harpoon gun crafted with Eitrite Bars. All harpoons fired by this weapon act like an Alkaline Harpoon, regardless of the harpoon type used as ammunition.

Its best modifiers are Godly and Demonic.
