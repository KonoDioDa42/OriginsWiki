Strange Tooth

The Strange Tooth is a pre-Hardmode accessory that is dropped by the Eye of Cthulhu in Master Mode. While equipped, the player will generate miniature Servants of Cthulhu to fight for them based on how many minion slots they have without actually taking up any slots. The Servants will kamikaze into enemies and have a chance to drop Hearts whenever the player is low on health. The Servants will slowly replenish over time.

Trivia

- The name minion buff for Strange Tooth's Servants is a nod to Eye-Brawl's line (from Skylanders: Giants) when using his "An Eye In Team" attack.
