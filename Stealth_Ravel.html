Stealth Ravel

Stealth Ravel is a Hardmode mount that is crafted using Putrid Scent and Ravel. Stealth Ravel turns the player into a small sphere that provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. The player's aggro is reduced by 400 while ravelled and this is equipped.
