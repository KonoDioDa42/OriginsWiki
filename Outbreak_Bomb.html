Outbreak Bomb

The Outbreak Bomb is an explosive crafted using Demonite Ore and Bombs. They are also sold by the Demolitionist NPC for 600 after selling him 110 Peat Moss once the Eater of Worlds has been defeated. Upon detonation, the bomb will release a cloud that inflicts Poisoned to any enemies that come into contact with it. If an enemy dies to this weapon while being poisoned, they will explode and inflict Poisoned to other nearby enemies.
