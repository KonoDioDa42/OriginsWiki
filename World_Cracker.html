World Cracker

The World Cracker is a pre-Hardmode, Riven-themed worm boss. It is summoned when three Gel Blisters are destroyed in a Riven world, or when a player uses Suspicious Looking Ice Cream anywhere in the Riven Hive or Underground Riven Hive.

The World Cracker has a very simple behavior, however, there are a few quirks that make it quite challenging. It has a total of 15/17/19 segments including the head and tail, and it all counts as one unlike the Eater of Worlds. It will circle around the player and move in the opposite direction of the player, making its movement unpredictable. While moving around it will shoot amoeba bubbles from any one of its segments that deals high damage. Furthermore, the World Cracker has armor on all of its segments that reduce damage until it is destroyed.

Defeating the World Cracker and destroying its armor are the only ways of obtaining Riven Carapace, and are one of the ways of obtaining usable quantities of Encrusted Ore alongside the Eye of Cthulhu. Defeating the World Cracker for the first time also allows the Tavernkeep NPC to spawn as the Unconcious Man, and allows meteorites to land.

Behavior

It behaves like most worms except that it prefers to attack in vertical strikes and moves opposite of the player. If the player doesn't move, it will instead go for a direct strike.

Attacks

  • Amoeba Bubble - The World Cracker will generate and be fire amoeba bubbles in bursts from any one of its segments. The timing of this attack is explained thoroughly in the 'Difficulty Changes' section below.
  • Frontal Beam - In Master Mode, the World Cracker will fire a Seam Beam-like laser out of its head in an attempt to cut the player off from where they are going. This attack deals great damage and inflicts 6% Riven Assimilation every hit. The World Cracker uses this attack every 5.47 seconds.

Notes

- The World Cracker will pursue the player even outside of the Hive.
- The World Cracker's health bar displays each of its segments' armor states with fresh and cracked versions to show how weakened it is.
- Similarly to the health bar, the actual armor sprites on the World Cracker change as it takes more damage.

Tips

- Focus on speed when preparing for the World Cracker as it attacks vertically, allowing you to easy sidestep it with enough acceleration.
- Moving really fast horizontally or keeping the World Cracker in the circular motion around you in harder difficulties reduces the likelihood of getting hit by its amoeba bubbles.

Trivia

- The Destroyer is the Hardmode mechanical boss equivalent of the World Cracker alongside the Eater of Worlds.
