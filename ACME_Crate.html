ACME Crate

The ACME Crate is a pre-Hardmode accessory created using Magic Tripwire and a Nitro Crate. Equipping the crate increases explosive blast radii by 60% and causes them to explode when an enemy is within proximity of the blast.

Tips

- The ACME Crate is very dangerous for the player to use due to the greatly increased blast radius it provides for explosives, increasing the likelihood of the player receiving self-damage. Due to this factor, it is adviseable players have some form of self-damage resistance if they lack the evasiveness to avoid their own explosives.

Trivia

- The ACME Crate is inspired by the fictional entity in Road Runner/Wile E. Coyote cartoon known as the ACME Corporation. Their products tend to have an explosive factor and a likelihood to pre-maturely detonate, hence the name for this item.
