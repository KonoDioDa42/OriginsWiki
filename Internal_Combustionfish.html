Internal Combustionfish

The Internal Combustionfish is an explosive that can be fished for in the Ashen Factory. It is currently unobtainable through conventional means, however, it can be obtained by using Shimmer. When thrown, it will home to nearby enemies and stick to them, only exploding after 5 seconds or when another enemy is nearby the stuck enemy. It will drop after detonation and can be infinitely reused.
