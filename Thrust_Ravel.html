Thrust Ravel

Thrust Ravel is a Hardmode, post-Plantera mount that is crafted using the Hoverboard and Ravel. Thrust Ravel turns the player into a small sphere, upon double-tapping the Down button, that provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. Thrust Ravel also allows the player to fly at a decent speed and for a longer duration than typical wings.
