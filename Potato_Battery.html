Potato Battery

The Potato Battery is a Hardmode accessory crafted using mech components dropped by the mechanical bosses. Equipping the Potato Battery causes all magic projectiles to home towards enemies. The Potato Battery talks to the player similarly to Lucy the Axe.

Dialogue

When any bird is nearby:

- "Agh! Bird! Bird! Kill it! It's evil!"

Notes

- The Potato Battery is capable of bending laser-like projectiles such as the Shadowbeam Staff or Seam Beam.
- Any magic weapons that already have homing projectiles while this is equipped simply have a stronger home-in on enemies.

Trivia

- The Potato Battery is a reference to the GLaDOS Potato Battery from the Portal 2 game.
- Nearly all lines of dialogue from the Potato Battery are ripped straight from GLaDOS' actual lines.
