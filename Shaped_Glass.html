Shaped Glass

The Shaped Glass is a boss-summoning consumable item that is crafted from Fiberglass Blocks and obtained by enemies found in the Fiberglass Undergrowth. Upon use, it will summon the Fiberglass Weaver. If not used in the Fiberglass Undergrowth, it will do nothing and will not be consumed.
