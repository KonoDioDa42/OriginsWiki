Bleeding Obsidian

Bleeding Obsidian is a type of Hardmode ore block found in the Dusk. It is the one of three ores that do not need to be turned into bars in order to utilize as a crafting material. Bleeding Obsidian requires at least 190% pickaxe power to mine, and yields the player 4-7 Bleeding Obsidian Shards when destroyed.

Tips

- The most efficent way to obtain Bleeding Obsidian is to convert regular Obsidian into it at an altar with a Soul of Night. An obsidian generator can be created to generate limitless amounts of Obsidian so long as the player has enough Souls of Night in their inventory.
