Focus Potion

The Focus Potion is a buff potion that can be crafted using Bottled Water, Fallen Star and Brineglow. When consumed, it provides the Mana Channeling buff, which causes the player to consume mana to boost weapon damage. This effect will last for 4 minutes, but can be canceled anytime by right-clicking the icon. The effect still works with magic weapons and just consumes more mana.
