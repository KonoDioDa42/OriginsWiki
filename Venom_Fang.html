Venom Fang

The Venom Fang is a Hardmodeaccessory that has a chance of being dropped by Carpalfish after ramming into a wall. The chance of repeat Venom Fangs dropping from continued rammings decreases. When equipped, all attacks from the player inflict Toxic Shock, which has a stronger effect while Acrid Armor is also equipped.
