Cirrhosis' Abhorrence

The Cirrhosis' Abhorrence is a pre-Hardmode accessory combining a Lousy Liver with a Messy Magma Leech. While equipped, 5 of the closest enemies to the player will have be constantly afflicted by the Bleeding, On Fire! and Rasterized. Furthermore, all attacks inflict Bleeding for 8 seconds and On Fire! for 2-6 seconds.
