Cryosten Armor

Cryosten armor is an armor set consisting of the Cryosten Helmet, Cryosten Breastplate and Cryosten Greaves. In order to craft the full set the player will need 135 Ice blocks, 3 Life Crystals and 12 Shiverthorn.

A full set of Cryosten armor provides the following benefits:

- +7 defense
- +1 HP/s life regeneration
- +12% max life
- +5% movement speed
- Increased movement speed on ice
Set bonus
- +1.5 HP/s for 60 ticks after picking up Hearts
- Immunity to Chilled and Frozen
