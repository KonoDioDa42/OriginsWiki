Urn of Ashes

The Urn of Ashes is a consumable item that can be crafted using Fungarust. Currently, the Urn of Ashes does not behave like other thrown powder and will not spread the Ashen or turn certain enemies into their Ashen counterparts, and is soley used to craft.
