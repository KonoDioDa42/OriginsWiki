Tendon Armor

Tendon armor is an armor set consisting of the Tendon Helmet, Tendon Shirt and Tendon Pants. In order to craft the full set the player will need 42 Crimtane Ore and 66 Vertebrae.

A full set of Tendon armor provides the following benefits:

- +10 defense
- +10% ranged damage
- +6% critical strike chance
- +20% ammo conservation
Set bonus
- Movement speed, acceleration/deceleration and jump speed are multiplied by the player's current life
