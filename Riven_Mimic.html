Riven Mimic

The Riven Mimic is a Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Underground Riven Hive. Riven Mimics are disguised as chests and rarely spawn in the Underground Hive, but can also be manually spawned by using a Key of Night. They behave like the other evil biome mimics.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Riven Mimic: "Mimics which evolve to the Riven Hive's conditions grow larger and more powerful as a result. They can be birthed from ordinary chests by force."
