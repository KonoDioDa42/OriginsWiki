Fiberglass Sword

The Fiberglass Sword is a pre-Hardmode broadsword that has a 1/10 (10%) chance to be dropped by the Fiberglass Weaver. Its primary attack has it being swung like a normal sword at a fast speed. Its alternate use, which can be triggered by pressing the Interact button allows it to be thrown like a boomerang.

Its best modifier is Legendary.
