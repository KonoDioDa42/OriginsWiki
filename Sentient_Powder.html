Sentient Powder

Sentient Powder is a consumable item that can be crafted using Acetabularia or purchased from the Dryad for 100 each during a Blood Moon in a Riven world. It is able to convert blocks and walls, including those of its world evil counterparts and Hallowed ones, into their Riven form. If thrown at a Bunny, Goldfish or Penguin, they will turn into their respective Riven variants: the Barnacle Bunny, Bottomfeeder, and Riven Penguin respectively.
