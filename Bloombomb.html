Bloombomb

The Bloombomb is an explosive that can be crafted using any Seeds and Bombs or pruchased from the Dryad NPC after completing her 'Clearing the Way' quest. Upon detonation, the Bloombomb will release 11-14 seeds that will bounce around briefly. It does not destroy tiles or any vegetation from its explosion.

Tips

- It is far cheaper to buy Bloombombs directly from the Dryad as opposed to crafting them, which will save the player 300 for every Bloombomb bought.
- Bloombombs are best used in enclosed areas where the seeds don't travel far away from the bomb's location.
