Bleeding Obsidian Armor

Bleeding Obsidian armor is a Hardmode armor set consisting of the Bleeding Obsidian Hardee, Bleeding Obsidian Trenchcoat and Bleeding Obsidian Chaps. In order to craft the full set the player will need the full Obsidian armor set and 9 Bleeding Obsidian blocks.

A full set of Bleeding Obsidian armor provides the following benefits:

- +26 defense
- +20% explosive velocity
- -25% self-damage
- +15% movement speed
- +2% acceleration/deceleration
Set bonus
- 50% chance to duplicate explosive projectiles
