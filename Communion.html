Communion

The Communion is a Hardmode magic weapon that has a 1/5 (20%) chance to be dropped by destroyed Defiled Mimics. The Communion fires a spark that homes towards enemies and beckons (pulls) them towards you and inflicting Rasterized for 0.58 seconds. It deals more damage depending on how many enemies it can bounce between, but suffers against enemies on their own.

The best modifier is Mythical.
