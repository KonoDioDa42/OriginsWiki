Bombardment

The Bombardment is a pre-Hardmode launcher that can be crafted using Defiled Bars and Undead Chunks, or dropped by Defiled Krushers. The Bombardment converts its ammunition into hovering explosives that remain in place for 7 seconds before exploding or coming in contact with an enemy. It also has a 50% chance not to consume ammo.

Its best modifier is Guileless.
