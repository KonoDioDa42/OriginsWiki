Dreadful Powder

Dreadful Powder is a consumable item that can be crafted using Soulspores or purchased from the Dryad for 100 each during a Blood Moon in a Defiled world. It is able to convert blocks and walls, including those of its world evil counterparts and Hallowed ones, into their Defiled form. If thrown at a Goldfish or Penguin, they will turn into their respective Defiled variants: the Shattered Goldfish and Bile Thrower respectively. However, when thrown at a Bunny, it will turn into a Defiled Mite.
