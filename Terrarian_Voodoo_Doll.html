Terrarian Voodoo Doll

The Terrarian Voodoo Doll is a pre-Hardmode accessory that is dropped by Skeletron in Master Mode. While equipped, the player's pickup range is increased. The dropped item also counts as wherever the player is located, meaning that if the doll is thrown in water it will count the player as in water at all times until moved. The item can additionally pick up items, Hearts and Stars with a slightly smaller range than the player, however, its own range is affected by any pickup range boosts as well.
