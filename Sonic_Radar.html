Sonic Radar

Sonic Radar is a pre-Hardmode accessory crafted using several materials found underground. It specifically allows the player to see valuables, enemies, and traps from a 20 tile radius.

Tips

Trivia

- Sonic Radar was originally meant to be an Expert Mode-exclusive drop from a Cubekon enemy known as the "Seeker".
