Bloodletter

The Bloodletter is a pre-Hardmode harpoon gun crafted with Crimtane Bars and a Harpoon Gun. Enemies struck by harpoons fired by this weapon are inflicted with Bleeding for 5 seconds. Additionally, any harpoons fired by the Bloodletter will home in on bleeding enemies.

Its best modifier is Godly or Demonic.
