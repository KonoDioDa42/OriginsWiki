Shardcannon

The Shardcannon is a pre-Hardmode gun that is sold by Arms Dealer for 10 after completing his 'Live Fire' quest. It also has a 1/5 (20%) chance of being obtained from Crunchy and Bilious Crates fished in the Defiled. The Kruncher acts like a rapid-firing shotgun, firing clusters of 3-5 shots at a time. It also has a unique sound effect from other weapons that it shares with its ancient version: the Ancient Kruncher.

Its best modifier is Unreal or Heavy Caliber.
