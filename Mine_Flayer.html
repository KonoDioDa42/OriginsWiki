Mine Flayer

The Mine Flayer is a Hardmode launcher crafted using mech components dropped by the mechanical bosses. When this weapon is swung, it will rapidly release 10 canisters at a 135-degree arc in front of the player at a decent velocity. It also has a 60% chance not to consume ammo.

Its best modifier is Guileless.

- It is most effective the closer the player is to the target, and while some form of self-damage protection is in use. This is so the player can prevent each of the released mines from being too spread out.
