Telephone Pole

The Telephone Pole is a Hardmode melee weapon crafted using Wood and Valkyrum Bars. When enemies are struck by this weapon, other nearby enemies will be struck by a shock chain for the same damage. The shock chain can go on infinitely across enemies.

Its best modifier is Legendary for either mode.
