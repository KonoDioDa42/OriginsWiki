Forbidden Voice

The Forbidden Voice is a pre-Hardmode accessory dropped by the Eater of Worlds in Master Mode. While the cursed version is equipped, the player can use the button to prevent any projectiles created by the player or enemies for 10 seconds. This active ability has a 15-second cooldown before it can be used again, and the player slowly builds up to 10% damage reduction while on cooldown. The damage reduction gained from the cooldown is permanent until the player uses the active ability again where it will need to redevelop after going on cooldown again.

While the uncursed version is equipped, damage is increased by 10% and size is increased by 20% for melee weapons instead.
