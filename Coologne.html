Coologne

Coologne is a Hardmode, Master Mode-exclusive accessory combining Frozen Turtle Shell and Mysterious Spray. When equipped, it inversely increases life regeneration as the player's health gets lower and applies 25% damage reduction when the player is below 50% of their max life. Like all of Mysterious Spray's variants, Coologne applies a unique visual effect on the player depending on their current health.

Notes

- Coologne's regeneration rate is inversely logarithmic to the player's current health.

- It is still possible to die with Mysterious Spray and its variants, by recieving quick bursts of damage similar to what it takes to slay a regenerating Defiled enemy.
