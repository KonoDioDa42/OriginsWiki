Bleeding Obsidian Kunai

The Bleeding Obsidian Kunai is a Hardmode throwing weapon that acts as a ranged counterpart to the Magic Dagger. The Bleeding Obsidian Kunai is a consumable weapon that replenishes itself after the thrown projectile is destroyed. The Kunai can be thrown more often depending on how many stacks of this item the player has, however, only a maximum of 8 stacks of this item can be reached. Enemies struck by this weapon are inflicted Bleeding for 5 seconds, Cursed Inferno for 2 seconds, Ichor for 3 seconds and Torn at 10% severity for 5 seconds.
