Explosive Artery

The Explosive Artery is a pre-Hardmode accessory that has a 1/87 (1.15%) chance of being dropped by a Blood Crawler or a Face Monster. While equipped, enemies inflicted with Bleeding within 24 tiles of the player have an 1/20 (5%) chance to explode every second.
