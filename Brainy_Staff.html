Brainy Staff

The Brainy Staff is a pre-Hardmode summon weapon that has a 1/17 (5.88%) chance of being dropped by Crimbrains. It summons a friendly Crimbrain minion that charges at enemies with a 10% chance of inflicting Confused for 1 second.

Brainy Staff's best modifer is Mythical.

Trivia

- Despite appearing with the same consistency as other Crimtane equipment, it is not craftable unlike the others.
